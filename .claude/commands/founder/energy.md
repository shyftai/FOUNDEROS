Manage founder energy, deep work blocks, and calendar structure.

Load `.claude/founderos/references/energy-management.md` before running.

Display:
```
<< FOUNDER:OS // ENERGY >>
```

Follow these steps:

1. Read current ENERGY.md
2. Determine the mode:
   - **Setup:** Create the weekly schedule template (first time)
   - **Check-in:** Review this week's energy and plan adjustments
   - **Audit:** Deep analysis of energy patterns over time
3. For **setup:**
   a. Ask: "What time do you do your best deep work?" (morning/afternoon)
   b. Ask: "How many hours of deep work per week do you want to protect?" (minimum 9 recommended)
   c. Ask: "Which day should be meeting-free?" (Wednesday recommended)
   d. Ask: "What are your non-negotiable recovery commitments?" (exercise, family, hobbies)
   e. Build the weekly schedule template
   f. Set delegation triggers — tasks the founder should stop doing
   g. Save to ENERGY.md
4. For **check-in:**
   a. Ask: "Energy level this week (1-10)?"
   b. Ask: "Deep work hours achieved?"
   c. Ask: "What drained your energy this week?"
   d. Ask: "What gave you energy?"
   e. Compare actual schedule vs ideal schedule
   f. Identify one specific adjustment for next week
   g. Check delegation triggers — is the founder doing work that should be delegated?
   h. Update ENERGY.md tracking table
5. For **audit:**
   a. Review energy tracking data over the past month/quarter
   b. Identify patterns:
      - Which days have highest energy?
      - What types of work drain vs energize?
      - Is deep work time being protected?
      - Are meetings being batched effectively?
   c. Cross-reference with decision quality from DECISIONS.md
      - Are bad decisions correlated with low energy periods?
   d. Suggest structural changes to the schedule
6. Display:

```

  │  Energy: {n}/10                                  │

  │  Meetings: {n}h                                  │
  │                                                  │
  │  Top drain: {what}                               │
  │  Top boost: {what}                               │
  │  Adjustment: {change for next week}              │
  │                                                  │
  └──────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```
