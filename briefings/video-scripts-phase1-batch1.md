## Production Notes

**Screen recording setup:**
- Dark terminal theme (recommend Dracula or One Dark)
- Font size large enough to read on mobile (18-20pt minimum)
- Record at 1080x1920 for vertical or 1920x1080 for horizontal
- Use a typing animation tool or type live for authenticity

**Voiceover style:**
- Conversational, not scripted-sounding
- Slightly fast pace — founder energy, not narrator energy
- No filler words but also no corporate polish
- Record in a quiet room, no music during demo sections

**Text overlays:**
- Sans-serif, bold, high contrast
- Bottom third of screen during hooks
- Keep on screen for 2-3 seconds minimum

**Editing:**
- Cut dead space between terminal outputs (speed up rendering)
- Add subtle zoom on key numbers (ARR, percentages, risk scores)
- No transitions — hard cuts only
- Optional: light ambient music in hook and CTA sections only
