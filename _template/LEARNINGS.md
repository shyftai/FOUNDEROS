# Learnings

Compound learning is the founder's ultimate advantage. Every win, miss, and experiment teaches something. Log it here.

## How to log

Every learning entry needs:
- **Date** — when it happened
- **Category** — strategy, decisions, delegation, energy, hiring, growth, fundraising, anti-learnings
- **Context** — what situation this came from
- **Insight** — the actual learning (one sentence)
- **Evidence** — what data or outcome supports this
- **Action** — what to do differently going forward
