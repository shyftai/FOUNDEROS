# Delegation Tracker

**Rule:** Only do what only you can do. If someone else can do it 70% as well, delegate.

## Delegation matrix

### Only the founder should do
- Vision and strategy decisions
- Key hires (final interview)
- Board and investor relationships
- Major partnership decisions
- Culture-defining moments

### Delegate with oversight
- Hiring process (delegate to HR:OS / hiring manager, founder does final round)
- Campaign strategy (delegate to GTM:OS, founder approves direction)
- Content strategy (delegate to CONTENT:OS, founder approves calendar)
- Financial planning (delegate to FINANCE:OS, founder reviews monthly)
- Community strategy (delegate to COMMUNITY:OS, founder checks health)

### Fully delegate
- Campaign execution (GTM:OS handles)
- Content creation and publishing (CONTENT:OS handles)
- Invoice processing (FINANCE:OS handles)
- Onboarding new hires (HR:OS handles)
- Community day-to-day engagement (COMMUNITY:OS handles)
- Investor CRM updates (INVESTOR:OS handles)
