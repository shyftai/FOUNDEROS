# Energy Management

**Rule:** Energy is finite. Protect deep work, batch meetings, guard recovery. A burned-out founder makes bad decisions.

## Deep work blocks

**Minimum deep work:** 3 blocks per week (3 hours each)
**Deep work rules:**
- No Slack, no email, no meetings during blocks
- Phone on Do Not Disturb
- Single task focus — one priority per block
- Best for: strategy, writing, product thinking, decision analysis

## Meeting rules

- **Batch meetings** — cluster on Tuesday/Thursday afternoons
- **Default meeting length:** 25 minutes (not 30), 50 minutes (not 60)
- **Meeting-free days:** Wednesday is sacred — no meetings
- **Say no by default** — "Does this meeting need me? Could it be an email?"
- **Prep required:** No meeting without an agenda. If no agenda, decline.

## Recovery protocols

- **Daily:** 30-minute break after every 3-hour deep work block
- **Weekly:** Full day off (no work communication)
- **Monthly:** One long weekend (3 days)
- **Quarterly:** Full week off after OKR close-out

## Delegation triggers

If you find yourself doing any of these, delegate immediately:
- [ ] Inbox management
- [ ] Scheduling meetings
- [ ] First-round interviews
- [ ] Slide deck formatting
- [ ] Data entry or CRM updates
- [ ] Routine reporting
- [ ] Social media posting
- [ ] Customer support (unless strategic)
